About Us

THELORD PUBLICATIONS LIMITED is a printing and publishing company located at H/No. 72 Matse Boi Street, GA-261 - 0817 Kaneshie, Accra, and registered under the company laws of Ghana since 2017.

We have extensive experience in printing and graphic design, offering printing services for a wide range of print media including:

Business Cards, Letterheads, Brochures, Annual Reports, Flyers, Books, Labels, Calendars, Magazines, Diaries, T-Shirts, Mugs, 3D Signs, Branding... and many more.

Our goal is to serve all the printing needs for each company we work with, as a one-stop-shop for all their marketing materials.

THELORD PUBLICATIONS LIMITED distinguishes itself through reliability, expertise, and competitive pricing.
